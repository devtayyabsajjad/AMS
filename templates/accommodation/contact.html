Frequently Asked Questions

How do I apply for an accommodation?

Simply browse our listings, select the accommodation that interests you, and click the "Apply" button. Fill out the application form with your details and submit it. The landlord will review your application and get back to you.

Is the platform free to use?

Yes! Harmony Housing is completely free for tenants to use. You can browse listings, apply to accommodations, and track your applications without any charges.

How long does it take to hear back from landlords?

Response times vary by landlord, but most respond within 24-48 hours. You can track the status of your application in your dashboard and will be notified when there's an update.

Can I modify or cancel my application?

Once submitted, applications cannot be edited, but you can contact support if you need to make changes or withdraw your application. We recommend reaching out to us as soon as possible.
